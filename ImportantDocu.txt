SSH key generation (if you hate typing login password all the time):
	cd ~/.ssh
	ssh-keygen rsa
	(don't set a password, just enter, enter, enter, enter, enter till key is generated)
	copy publickey (usually id_rsa.pub) to remote ~/.ssh
